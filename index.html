What is Anime?

Anime is is a style of Japanese animated entertainment that has become increasingly popular around the world in recent years.

"The word anime is a shortened form of the Japanese word animÄ“shon, which means "animation." While anime has its roots in Japan, it has since spread to other countries, and today, anime can be found in many different languages, including English."
More Information

Anime is often characterized by colorful artwork, fantastical themes, and vibrant characters.

Famous Anime

    Popular Genres

    Isekai
    "alternative world" anime, which covers a broad range of storylines in which a character is transported into a new life
    Shoujo
    Intended primarily for girls.
    Josei
    Josei animes are usually targeted towards older, adult women.
    Mecha
    Mecha is a genre of Japanese manga and anime that heavily features or focuses on mechanical innovation.
